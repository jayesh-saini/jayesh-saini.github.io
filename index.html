Full House

Full House is the Grand prize in any Tambola game played. It means all 15 Numbers of a ticket are cut, striked out. If the audience is large than host keeps it as multiple like Full House 1, Full House 2 etc. A single tambola ticket consists of 15 numbers, so when all 15 are striked it's a full house.
